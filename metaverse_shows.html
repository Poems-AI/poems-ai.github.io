Inspired by new technologies and experiences with themselves, Marcos de la Fuente wrote the collection of poems called Metarelity in 2021, he talks about metaverses, cryptocurrencies and non-fungible elements among other new terminologies. To take these poems to more sensory levels, we trained an artificial intelligence to generate images from the interpretations of the poems. In this space, in addition to finding the poems, there is access to the AI visualizations.
